FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. How can I add my own handwriting?

  • To use your handwriting, generate a font of your handwriting.
  • Websites like Calligraphr let you do that.
  • Once you get the .ttf file of your handwriting, upload it using the 'Upload your handwriting font' button in the customizations section.

2. Where can I get more fonts?

You can use fonts from Quantum Enterprises Font Vault.

3. There are gaps between letters in custom fonts

This is a known issue. Adjust the letter and word spacing options to reduce the gaps.
